Welcome to FlickSight

FlickSight is a customer feedback management software that assists businesses in gathering and analyzing customer input. Our platform provides meaningful insights based on company data and customer interactions, allowing businesses to discover and address areas for improvement.

Here are our main goals:
  1. Help businesses gain a better understanding of their customers' needs and preferences by providing insightful customer feedback analysis.
  2. Empower businesses to take action based on customer feedback to improve their products, services, and overall customer support.
  3. Provide a comprehensive customer feedback management solution that is user-friendly and integrates with various systems to streamline the feedback collection and analysis process.

Features

FlickSight offers a range of powerful features designed to help businesses improve their customer experience by offering guidance and analyzing customer feedback. With FlickSight, businesses can easily gather customer feedback from a variety of channels, including email, website, and CRMs. Our software automatically analyzes customer feedback and presents it in a clear and concise format, offering actionable insights

  • Customer feedback collection through various channels (email, website, CRM, etc.).
  • Actionable insights based on customer feedback to identify areas for improvement.
  • Guidance and tips for common customer success issues and strategies.
  • Customizable feedback forms to tailor the feedback collection process to the business's needs.

Our Solution

FlickSight offers a unique customer feedback management solution that sets it apart from the competition. Our software provides businesses with real-time insights into customer sentiment and feedback, allowing them to quickly identify and address issues. Additionally, our mobile app provides guidance to businesses on common customer support issues, making it a comprehensive solution for improving customer satisfaction. With FlickSight, businesses can take a proactive approach to customer feedback management and improve their overall customer satisfaction strategy.
